Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how GetComparley collects, uses, and protects your information.

We collect search queries to fetch product data from affiliate partners like CJ.com and eBay. Your data is used solely to display comparison results and is not stored or shared with third parties beyond what’s required for API requests.

For eBay integration, we use OAuth to authenticate requests, requiring your consent to access product data on your behalf. You can revoke this access at any time via eBay’s settings.

We use cookies to improve user experience, such as remembering your selected stores. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

For questions, contact us via our website.
